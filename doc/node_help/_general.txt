Welcome to the ulnoiot environment.

Try the following commands:
- wifi("name","pw") connects to wifi, wscan scans, wip shows ip address
- reset() or reboot() to reset the board
- help, help(), man, or man() shows this again

Also make sure to make use of tab-completion (just hit the tab  key
once or twice to see possible commands).

With help("command_name") or man("command_name") you can inquire about the
following commands:
!!help_list_print(command_list())

Go to http://iot.ulno.net for more information.
